Breaded Air Fryer Chicken

Description

Delicious chicken floured and fried in the air fryer!

Ingredients

  • Large Chicken Breast
  • Tablespoon of oil
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs (or other method of breading)
  • 1 Teaspoon of your combined seasonings

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts in bowl and drizzle with oil
  2. Mix breadcrumbs with seasoning in a seperate dish
  3. Coat chicken breasts in bread crumbs dish
  4. Air fry in the air fryer at 390 degrees Farenheit for 10-12 minutes
  5. After 7 minutes, open the air fryer and flip the chicken
