Kalbi Korean Short Ribs

Description

Delicious Kalbi Korean Short Ribs!

Ingredients

  • 5-6 pounds of short ribs
  • 4 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seed, toasted
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • green onions chopped fine
  • 2-3 tablespoon brown sugar or honey

Steps

  1. Soak meat for at least half a day in the marinade
  2. Cook over charcoal
