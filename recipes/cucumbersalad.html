Soy Sauce Cucumber Salad

Description

Cucumber Salad, drizzled with soy sauce (or shoyu) and vinegar!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds cucumbers
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 clove of minced garlic

Steps

  1. Slice up the cucumbers into bite size pieces
  2. Add all other ingredients together and pour over the cucumbers
  3. Refrigerate for at least two hours, then it is ready to eat!
